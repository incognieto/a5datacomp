The Project

Here are some of the projects we've undertaken as part of our coursework in the D4 Informatics Engineering program at Politeknik Negeri Bandung. Each project showcases our dedication to learning, creativity, and teamwork:

  • dompetQ (C++)

    dompetQ is a money tracking program developed in C++. It helps users manage their finances by tracking income and expenses efficiently.

  • dompetQ (Python)

    The Python version of dompetQ offers an intuitive interface and enhanced features for tracking personal finances, making budgeting easier than ever.

  • komiqinAja

    komiqinAja is a scrapes data from MyAnimeList, allowing users to view rankings, and other details about various manga and anime series.

  • datacomp

    datacomp is a scrapes data from Tokopedia to compare the performance of different stores selling computer components.
